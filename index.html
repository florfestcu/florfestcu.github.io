FAQs

  • Why should I attend flor fest?

    flor fest is a new concept for a festival to unite all of the local DIY/emo bands from the Midwest area for a festival. It will serve as a fun environment for high school and college students to enjoy great music. Majority of the profit will go towards The Trevor Project (a hotline for LGBTQ+ members to get help from professional counselors).

  • Safety at flor fest

    We want every fan to feel safe at the festival. Our Safety Page is the place to find our anti-harassment policy, get tips to come prepared, learn where and how to find help at the festival, and read about security measures we have in place. Specific questions regarding safety, security, medical, and any others can be directed to admin@florfestcu.com. If you have an immediate need while on-site seek out any festival staffer, police, fire, or security.

  • What are the transportation methods to the festival?

    - Public Transportation via MTD buses (recommended due to limited parking)
    - Driving (Effective only in Groups of 4+ concert goers)
    - Uber & Lyft (Fast & Reliable)

  • Will I be able to buy band and festival merch?

    Yes! You can find band merch for any bands that decide to participate in our sales. We will only be taking Venmo and Cash for band merch sales. For festival merch, it is yet to be determined how many t-shirts will be ordered for the festival beforehand :(. We'll keep you all updated!

  • Will there be alcoholic drinks served?

    Unfornately, we are not going to be able to receive a liquor license for the first year of our festival. However, we won't stop you from bringing booze yourself if you're 21+.

  • When will my merch order ship out?

    Most merch orders will printed out within 3 business days
